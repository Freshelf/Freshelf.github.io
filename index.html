How the product works?

  • At register, scan QR code to link the app to the register directly
  • As foods are scanned, they also appear in your app, removing the need to manually scan them yourself
  • Displays a picture of your product (taken from google), name, and expiry date in app
  • If the food item is fresh produce, an estimated expiry date is used
  • If you scan something that is not food (ex. you’re also buying a magazine), the app omits it into another category (but you can add the magazine back into the main selection if desired)

Prices

Free:

  • Auto-sort by expiry date
  • Sort by food category (ex. dairy, fruits, vegetables...)
  • Lists the quanity of every food item


Paid:

  • Provides recipe suggestions based on inventory
  • Recipe suggestions account for dietary restrictions
  • Shows grocery brochures for stores near you
  • Sync across different devices
