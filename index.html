Gone are the days of rotten avocados and moldy bread filling up the limited space in your pantry. Our smart application keeps track of all your food items and alerts you when the expiry date is fast approaching.
With custom suggestions of mouth-watering recipes, you’ll never have to think twice (or sniff twice) before cooking up some of your favourite meals.


How the product works?...


Here at Freshelf, our number one priority is to help you stop losing money from wasted food. Our easy-to-use app is filled with efficient tracking algorithms to help you organize and live a fresher life.
At the supermarket, just scan your phone at the register and all the food that you purchased will be automatically added to our Freshelf app! Listed are the expiry dates, date of purchase, quantity of item, and many other features tailored to your needs. Let us help you reduce your food waste and plan better.


Product Features

Free:

  • Auto-sort by expiry date
  • Sort by food category (ex. dairy, fruits, vegetables...)
  • Lists the quanity of every food item

Paid:

  • Provides recipe suggestions based on inventory
  • Recipe suggestions account for dietary restrictions
  • Shows grocery brochures for stores near you
  • Sync across different devices
