Freshelf will categorize and sort your items by expiry dates, notify you when the expirations dates are closing by. Freshelf also offers diverse customization options, recipes suggestions, grocery brochures, syncing across various devices, and that's only for the main features!


How does it work?...


First of all, you scan the QR code with the app at the cashier register. Then as the items are scanned they will be displayed on the app. Finally everything will be organized into different categories so you'll be able to keep track of them easily!

Product Features

Free:

  • Categorizing and sort your items by expiry dates
  • expiry dates notifications
  • basic customization features

Paid:

  • Recipes suggestion based on your inventory and your dietary needs
  • Syncing across various devices
  • Daily grocery brochures
  • Advanced customization features
